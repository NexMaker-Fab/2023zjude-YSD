### How to access Fusion 360
- First, open the [Autodesk](https://www.autodesk.com.cn/products/fusion-360/overview).

<div align="center">
  <img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/erkoww/YSD_img/img/%E5%B1%8F%E5%B9%95%E6%88%AA%E5%9B%BE%202023-10-18%20152902.png" width = "800"/>
</div>

- Second, apply for student accreditation according to the Guidelines.

<div align="center">
  <img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/erkoww/YSD_img/img/20231018160426.png" width = "800"/>
</div>

<div align="center">
  <img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/erkoww/YSD_img/img/20231018161152.png" width = "800"/>
</div>

<div align="center">
  <img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/erkoww/YSD_img/img/20231018160426.png" width = "800"/>
</div>

- Third, wait for qualification approval.

- Fourth, download Fusion 360 desktop edition.

<div align="center">
  <img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/erkoww/YSD_img/img/%E5%B1%8F%E5%B9%95%E6%88%AA%E5%9B%BE%202023-10-18%20161811.png" width = "800"/>
</div>

- Fifth, use your Autodesk ID to log in Fusion 360.

- Sixth, invite your team members to join.

- Seventh, start online collaboration.
